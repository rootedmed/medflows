Comprehensive Diabetes Management Tool

This tool is designed for individuals with Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes to manage their condition effectively.

Features

  • Daily Blood Sugar Tracking
  • Insulin Dosage Calculator
  • Diet and Nutrition Guide
  • Exercise Recommendations
  • Status Reports and Analytics

Daily Blood Sugar Tracking

Record your daily blood sugar levels and monitor trends over time. This feature allows you to set reminders for regular checks.

Insulin Dosage Calculator

Calculate your insulin dosage based on your carbohydrate intake and current blood sugar levels. Ensure to consult with your healthcare provider.

Diet and Nutrition Guide

Access a comprehensive guide on the best foods for diabetes management, meal plans, and healthy recipes.

Exercise Recommendations

Find tailored exercise routines that help manage blood sugar levels and improve overall health.

Status Reports and Analytics

Generate reports on your blood sugar levels, insulin usage, and diet, helping you and your healthcare provider make informed decisions.
