Understanding Hypertension

Hypertension, or high blood pressure, is a chronic medical condition wherein the blood pressure in the arteries is persistently elevated. This condition can lead to various health complications.

Management Strategies

  • Lifestyle changes: Diet, Exercise, Stress Management
  • Medication: Different classes of antihypertensive medications
  • Regular monitoring: Keeping track of blood pressure readings
