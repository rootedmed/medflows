Comprehensive Hyperlipidemia and ASCVD Prevention Management Tool

Overview

This tool is designed to assist healthcare providers in managing patients with hyperlipidemia and assessing their risk for Atherosclerotic Cardiovascular Disease (ASCVD).

Management Guidelines

  • Assess patient risk factors including age, gender, cholesterol levels, blood pressure, diabetes status, and smoking history.
  • Consider lifestyle modifications (diet, exercise, smoking cessation).
  • Evaluate the need for pharmacotherapy based on ASCVD risk assessment.
